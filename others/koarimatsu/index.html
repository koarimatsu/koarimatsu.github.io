小惑星 (27590) Koarimatsu

命名理由

(27590) Koarimatsu = 2000 YO132

Ko Arimatsu (b. 1987) is an astronomer at Kyoto University. He discovered a kilometer-sizedKuiper belt object by stellar occultation using a low-cost small telescope named the OrganizedAutotelescopes for Serendipitous Event Survey or OASES.

出典: International Astronomical Union / WG Small Body Nomenclature
